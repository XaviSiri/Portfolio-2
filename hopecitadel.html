About Us

Our work at HOPE Citadel Foundation is supported by a dedicated team of volunteers across all our programme areas as well as administrative and logical roles. Given the nature of our work, no monetary value has been attributed to their work. The tasks undertaken would, if it not performed by volunteers, require the employment of paid personnel.

Our Beginning

The Hope Citadel Foundation was founded in 2004 by Mr. & Mrs. Otwala. Creating a Christian medical charity was the founders' goal. Over the years, door-to-door evangelism, one-on-one evangelism, and/or counseling for individuals at the medical camp have complemented our medical outreaches.

Our Mission

To carry the banner of Christ to all peoples through our outreach missions and to be a driving force recognized for continuously improving platforms for inclusive development.

Our Vision

We seek a world where the love of Christ overcomes despair and people live with hope and dignity.

Our Mandate

We are committed to continually enhancing the less fortunate people's spiritual, physical, and socioeconomic well-being.

Our Plans

Our medical outreaches aim to offer specialized medical services to all communities we partner with. Through general outpatient, dental services, cervical cancer screening, minor and major surgeries, all of which are complimented with an array of services which include an on-site lab and a fully stocked pharmacy. All the services we offer to the communities come free of charge.
